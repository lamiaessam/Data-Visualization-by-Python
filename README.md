I’m excited to share my latest practice project in Data Visualization with Python. I focused on turning raw data into clean, easy-to-read visual stories using different types of charts.
Here is a quick look at the charts I built from scratch:
🔹 Line & Scatter Plots: To track trends over time (like daily weather changes). 
🔹 Bar Charts (Vertical & Horizontal): To easily compare sales performance across different countries. 
🔹 Dual-Axis Chart: An advanced layout that overlays both Sales and Profit together in one view. 
🔹 Pie Charts: To show a beautiful percentage breakdown of monthly home expenses.
Every chart reminded me that good visualization is all about making complex numbers simple and intuitive at a single glance.
   Tech Stack: Python (Matplotlib & Seaborn).
